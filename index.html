The American Revolutionary War

The Revolutionary War (American Revolutionary War) started in 1775 and ended in 1783. Skirmishes ( British men vs. colonial milita men) started in April 1775 and by the next summer then were in all out war. Then, France entered on the side of the colonists. Since France entered the war was now international. With Frane by our side Britain surrendered in Yorktown, Virgina on October 1781. Although the fighting didn't end till 1783.

The Vote For War

The Secould Continental Congress voted in Philidephia to make a Continental Army. They also decided to have George Washington as the head. On June 17, 1775 the first major battle happened, the Battle of Bunker Hill.
